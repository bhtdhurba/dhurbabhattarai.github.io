Research Store · Klaviyo · Email

Rebuilding a Welcome Series to Increase Revenue Per Recipient

The Challenge

Default single-email welcome flow with no segmentation, generic copy, and no product education — resulting in poor engagement and zero attributed revenue.

The Objective

Rebuild the welcome series into a 4-email sequence that educates, builds trust, and converts first-time subscribers into buyers within 7 days.

My Approach

Designed a 4-email flow with value-first content, social proof, and a time-limited offer. Split tested subject lines across email 1 and email 3.

Key Takeaway

A structured welcome series with a single clear CTA per email consistently outperforms generic multi-offer sequences for new subscriber conversion.
